Johnny Cash

The Man in Black

A BRIEF SUMMARY OF AN ICON

  • Feb 1932 - Born in Kingsland, Arkansas.
  • May 1944 - Johnny, then known as J.R., loses his brother to a table saw.
  • 1950 - Johnny enlists in the US Army, drops JR for Johnny, and develops an addiction to alcohol and drugs.
  • Aug 1954 - After an honorable discharge, Johnny marries his first wife, Vivian Liberto.
  • 1955 - The first break of his career comes as he records "Hey Porter" at Sun Records.
  • May 1955 - Roseanne Cash is born.
  • 1957 - The popular "Walk the Line" hits the charts at No. 1.
  • 1965 - The first of many arrests after being picked up for possession.
  • 1966 - Johnny's drug abuse and infidelity lead to divorce.
  • 1967 - Johnny and June accept a Grammy for "Jackson".
  • Oct 1967 - A suicide attempt leads a drug-addled Johnny to an epiphany.
  • Mar 1968 - Johnny marries June.
  • Mar 1970 - John Carter Cash is born.
  • 1975 - Johnny writes his first autobiography "Man in Black".
  • 2002 - Johnny covers Nine Inch Nails' "Hurt", leading to a 2003 Grammy.
  • May 2003 - June Carter dies after complications of heart surgery.
  • Sep 2003 - The Man in Black joins June in the afterlife.
